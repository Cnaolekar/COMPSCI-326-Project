Workout Plan

Full Body Workout Routine (8 Weeks)

This is a 3-days per week workout plan designed to build strength and muscle.

Weeks 1-2: Foundation
  • Squats: 3 sets x 12 reps
  • Push-ups: 3 sets x 15 reps
  • Bent-over Rows: 3 sets x 12 reps
  • Plank: 3 sets x 30 seconds
Weeks 3-4: Progression
  • Deadlifts: 4 sets x 10 reps
  • Dumbbell Bench Press: 4 sets x 10 reps
  • Pull-ups/Assisted Pull-ups: 4 sets x 8 reps
  • Russian Twists: 4 sets x 15 reps per side
Weeks 5-6: Intensification
  • Lunges: 3 sets x 10 reps per leg
  • Overhead Press: 3 sets x 12 reps
  • Single-Arm Dumbbell Rows: 3 sets x 10 reps per arm
  • Leg Raises: 3 sets x 15 reps
Weeks 7-8: Max Strength
  • Front Squats: 5 sets x 5 reps
  • Weighted Dips: 5 sets x 5 reps
  • One-Arm Dumbbell Snatch: 5 sets x 5 reps per arm
  • Cable Woodchoppers: 5 sets x 10 reps per side
